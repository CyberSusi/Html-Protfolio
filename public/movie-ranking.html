My Top Movies

The best movies for a night in.

The Net

A thrilling movie about a computer programmer who stumbles upon a conspiracy that puts her life in danger.
Starring Sandra Bullock, this film combines suspense and technology in an unforgettable way.

I Care A Lot

A dark comedy about a legal guardian who exploits her elderly clients for financial gain, only to meet her match in a dangerous adversary.
Starring Rosamund Pike, this movie is both gripping and thought-provoking.

Kiki's Delivery Service

A heartwarming animated film about a young witch who starts her own delivery service in a new town.
This Studio Ghibli classic is filled with charm, adventure, and beautiful animation.
